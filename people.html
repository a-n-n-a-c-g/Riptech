Meet the Leadership Team

“I thought home security would be just another headache, but RipTech made it easy and understandable.” -Jerry, California


Jomel Panoga

President and CEO

Jomel heads RipTech as President and CEO.
He recently finished an MBA after returning to school at the age of 47. His work at RipTech is informed by a long career with Microsoft, which he left six months ago in order to work closer to where his three children go to school and fulfill his dream of owning his own business.


Lina Cardoza Cassler

Chief Technology and Information Officer

Lina keeps internal company systems streamlined and efficient, as well as developing groundbreaking technologies and systems that keep RipTech at the forefront of the home security industry. After earning her Masters degree in computer programming in her home country of Mexico, Lina moved to the United States to marry her husband, Joseph. She credits her 3 years with Adobe, Inc. in Mexico with giving her a love for system development. Lina’s greatest joys are her husband and their 2 year-old daughter Lizeth.





Tim Shawcroft

Assistant Technology Officer

Tim provides support from his position as Assistant Technology Officer.
Behind the scenes of RipTech, Tim’s brilliance with both hard and soft ware helped RipTech find its footing as a startup. Now, as a qualified Physical Security Professional, Tim helps ensure that each home with RipTech’s state-of-the-art technology is secure.


Annie Deulen

Chief of Operations

At 23, Annie is the youngest Chief of Operations at a technology company. Annie won a national competition through her work as a computer programmer at her university while earning her degree, drawing the attention of many in the field. Despite receiving very competitive offers from larger companies, Annie decided to join RipTech. “I always prefer to do what people don’t expect, and choosing RipTech was definitely unexpected. But I like the idea of a smaller company that leads the market but keeps the hometown feel.”

David Scott

Chief Strategy and Innovation Officer

David serves as Chief Strategy and Innovation Officer for RipTech. A busy college senior studying computer programming, David has released several games for iOS and has a new app in development. When he isn’t studying or working, he enjoys attending his girlfriend’s classical music recitals and watching Netflix.
