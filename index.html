About Riptech

Our founder, Scott Anderson, was attempting to go on family vacation. He walked through the whole house, turning off lights and air conditioning, while alarming the rooms. They were running slightly late, but more so when his daughter announced she'd forgotten her favorite shoes..and the whole process had to be undone and re-done. Finally, at the airport, wondering if he'd locked the back door, Scott hoped that his dog, Ripper would keep the house safe. He pulled out his phone and had sudden inspiration: what if you could control your home from your phone? And so Riptech was born.

“My husband and I had stopped going on vacation. He is a lawyer, and, with my home staging business, there are too many important papers and valuable objects in our home. I could never feel good about leaving. After getting set up with PadLock and Watchdog, we finally felt like we could go on vacation. Last month was our 30th anniversary and we went on a trip for the first time in five years! Anytime I felt even a little nervous, I checked my phone and could see that there was nothing wrong. I love how easy the system is, even for technology novices like me. My husband even says RipTech saved our marriage. :)”
–Karen, California
